Pros

  • Massive 6000mAh battery lasts two days
  • 100W charging: 0-100% in 30 minutes
  • Excellent Hasselblad-tuned cameras
  • QHD+ display with perfect calibration
  • IP68 + IP69 dust/water resistance

Cons

  • Large and heavy at 210g
  • OxygenOS still has some bloatware
  • No headphone jack or microSD slot
  • Telephoto only 3x (vs 5x on competitors)

Battery Champion

The OnePlus 13's 6000mAh battery is a game-changer. In our testing, it consistently lasted a full two days of moderate use, and even power users will comfortably get through a full day. Combined with 100W SUPERVOOC charging that takes the battery from 0 to 100% in just 30 minutes, battery anxiety is a thing of the past.

Camera Quality

The Hasselblad partnership continues to pay dividends. The triple 50MP camera system produces images with excellent color accuracy and dynamic range. The LYT-808 main sensor captures stunning detail, and the Hasselblad color profiles add a distinctive, pleasing look to photos.
