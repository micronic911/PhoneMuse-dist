Photography Powerhouse

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra's camera system is its crown jewel. The Leica-tuned triple camera setup delivers professional-grade results, with the 200MP periscope telephoto producing jaw-dropping zoom shots at up to 5x optical magnification. The LYT-900 main sensor captures exceptional detail and dynamic range.

Performance

Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite, the Xiaomi 15 Ultra delivers top-tier performance across the board. Gaming, multitasking, and AI features all run flawlessly. The vapor chamber cooling system does an excellent job of managing thermals during extended gaming sessions.
