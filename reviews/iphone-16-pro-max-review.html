Apple refines the formula with stellar battery life and the most capable iPhone camera system yet.

Pros

  • Incredible all-day battery life
  • A18 Pro chip sets performance records
  • Beautiful ProMotion OLED display
  • Excellent 5x optical zoom camera
  • Outstanding video recording capabilities

Cons

  • 27W charging is painfully slow
  • 8GB RAM feels limiting for future-proofing
  • Heavy at 227g
  • Still no USB-C 3.0 on base iPhones

Design

The iPhone 16 Pro Max is a refined evolution rather than a revolution. The titanium frame is lighter and more durable, while the new 6.9-inch display features even slimmer bezels. The Ceramic Shield front and frosted glass back give the device a premium feel that's unmistakably Apple.

Performance

The A18 Pro chip continues Apple's tradition of leading mobile performance benchmarks. Real-world performance is buttery smooth, with iOS 18's animations feeling incredibly polished. The chip also enables advanced Apple Intelligence features that run entirely on-device.
