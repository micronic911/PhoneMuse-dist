Camera Excellence

The Pixel 9 Pro's triple camera system delivers outstanding results in virtually every scenario. The 50MP main sensor captures rich detail and natural colors, while the 48MP ultrawide and 48MP telephoto round out a versatile system. But it's Google's computational photography that truly sets this phone apart.

AI Features

With 16GB of RAM, the Pixel 9 Pro handles Google's growing suite of AI features with ease. Magic Editor, Best Take, Audio Magic Eraser, and the new Gemini-powered assistant all work seamlessly. These features feel genuinely useful rather than gimmicky.
