Xiaomi 17 Confirmed to Launch in India Next Month

July 2, 2026·3 min read

Xiaomi has officially confirmed that its latest flagship smartphone, the Xiaomi 17, will launch in India next month. The device will be powered by Qualcomm's latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset and feature Leica-tuned cameras.

India Launch Details

The Xiaomi 17 will be available in India starting August 15, 2026, with prices expected to start around ₹54,999 for the base 12GB/256GB variant. Xiaomi will offer the device through its official website, Mi stores, and Amazon India.

Key Specifications

The Xiaomi 17 features a 6.55-inch LTPO AMOLED display with QHD+ resolution and 120Hz refresh rate. Under the hood, it's powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset paired with up to 16GB of LPDDR6 RAM and 512GB of UFS 5.0 storage.

The camera system includes a 50MP Leica-tuned main sensor with variable aperture, a 50MP ultrawide camera, and a 50MP telephoto with 3x optical zoom. The battery is rated at 5500mAh with 120W wired and 50W wireless charging support.
